Why Mental Health Matters

Overall Well-being

Mental health is fundamental to our collective and individual ability to think, express emotions, interact with others, earn a living, and enjoy life.

Physical Health Connection

Mental health directly impacts physical health, affecting everything from heart health to immune system response.

Quality of Life

Good mental health contributes to better relationships, work performance, and ability to cope with life's challenges.
