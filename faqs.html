Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Diagnodose

Is Diagnodose a replacement for a doctor?

No, Diagnodose is not a replacement for professional medical care. While our platform can help you understand your symptoms and provide initial guidance for minor health concerns, it should be used as a preliminary tool only. For any serious medical conditions, persistent symptoms, or if you're unsure about your health, always consult with a qualified healthcare professional.

How accurate are the symptom analysis results?

Our symptom analysis system uses our ingenious database and is regularly updated with medical knowledge. However, it should be considered as a guide rather than a definitive diagnosis. The accuracy depends on the information provided and the complexity of symptoms. Always verify results with a healthcare professional.

Is my health information secure?

Yes, we take your privacy very seriously. All your health information is encrypted and stored securely following industry-standard security protocols. We comply with healthcare data protection regulations and never share your personal information without your explicit consent.

How can I find a doctor through Diagnodose?

Our "Find a Doctor" feature allows you to search for healthcare professionals in your area. You can filter by specialty, location, and availability. We verify all listed medical professionals to ensure they have valid credentials and licenses.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

In case of a medical emergency, do NOT wait to use Diagnodose. Immediately call your local emergency services or go to the nearest emergency room. Diagnodose is not designed for emergency situations.
